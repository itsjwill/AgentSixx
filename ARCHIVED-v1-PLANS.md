## Why We Cut Features

From the v2 strategic plan:

> "The original doc had 17+ features across lead gen, nurture, marketing, content, and reporting. Shipping all of that would take 4-6 months and each feature would be mediocre."

> "v1 ships three features, excellent."

> "Why this cut: three features shipped well beats seventeen shipped badly. Each v1 feature has a clear ROI story an agent can verify against their own bank statement inside 60 days."
