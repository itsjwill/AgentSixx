## The Pitch (One Sentence)

> **The only TCPA-compliant AI outreach system for real estate agents, set up and operated for you, replacing your ISA team at a fraction of the cost.**

**NOT the pitch:** "Respond to leads in 5 seconds." Everyone promises that. It's commodity.

**The REAL wedge is two things nobody combines:**
1. Professional compliance infrastructure (10DLC, DNC scrub, litigator list, consent pages, TCPA E&O insurance)
2. AI Voice replacing a human ISA team at 70-83% less cost

## Risks (Honest)

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| TCPA exposure is existential | E&O insurance BEFORE first client |
| AI voice quality is make-or-break | 50 test calls, backup plan (SMS-only) |
| n8n breaks at ~20 clients | Plan v2 rebuild starting at client 10 |
| Fulfillment labor is scaling wall | Pricing supports 2 hrs/client/mo |
| Agent churn at day 90 | 90-day guarantee forces honest sales |
| Solo founder bottleneck | Accept 70-hr weeks for first 90 days |
| AI commoditizing fast | Compliance moat is durable, voice is not |
