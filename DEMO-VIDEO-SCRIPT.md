# AgentSix Demo Video - Production Guide

> **Duration:** 90 Seconds (1:30) | **Goal:** Convert Real Estate Agents

---

## Why 90 Seconds?

| Duration | Problem | Best For |
|----------|---------|----------|
| 60s | Too rushed, can't show AI transcript | Ads only |
| **90s** | **Sweet spot - shows all "wow" moments** | **Landing page hero** |
| 2:00+ | Drop-off increases | "Full demo" click |

### What 90s Allows That 60s Doesn't:
- ✅ AI Call Transcript (proves AI sounds natural)
- ✅ Analytics/ROI section (847% ROI closes deals)
- ✅ Breathing room (doesn't feel rushed)

---

## Video Versions to Create

| Version | Duration | Purpose | Placement |
|---------|----------|---------|-----------|
| **Hero Video** | 90s | Primary conversion | Landing page |
| YouTube Ad | 30s | Awareness | Pre-roll ads |
| Facebook Ad | 30-45s | Engagement | Feed ads |
| Instagram Reel | 30s | Viral reach | Reels/Stories |

---

## Dashboard Pages Used

| # | Page | Section | Time | Purpose |
|---|------|---------|------|---------|
| 1 | `/dashboard` | Stats + Live Activity | 7s | "It's working right now" |
| 2 | `/dashboard/appointments` | Calendar view | 8s | "Calendar fills itself" |
| 3 | `/dashboard/calls` | AI Transcript | 12s | "AI sounds natural" |
| 4 | `/dashboard/compliance` | Score + Blocked count | 10s | "Zero TCPA risk" |
| 5 | `/dashboard/analytics` | ROI + Funnel | 10s | "847% ROI proof" |

### Pages NOT Used (too complex for 90s):
- ❌ `/leads` - AI scoring needs explanation
- ❌ `/sms` - 6-stage funnel too detailed
- ❌ `/conversations` - Save for sales call
- ❌ `/settings` - Boring configuration
- ❌ `/help` - Implies difficulty
