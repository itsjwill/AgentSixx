# Core Features Deep Dive

## Feature 1: Intelligent Voice Agent

### What It Does
A conversational AI that handles calls like your best employee — qualifying leads, booking appointments, and knowing when to transfer to you.

### How It Works

```
INBOUND CALL FLOW:
─────────────────
Ring → AgentSix answers instantly → Greets caller → 
Identifies need → Qualifies (timeline, budget, motivation) → 
Books appointment OR transfers to you live

OUTBOUND CALL FLOW:
──────────────────
Lead enters system → Compliance check passes → 
Call scheduled (optimal time based on timezone) → 
AgentSix calls → Natural conversation → 
Books appointment / Schedules callback / Marks DNC
```

### Sample Call Script

```
AgentSix: "Hi, this is Sarah from ABC Realty. I saw you were 
          looking at homes in the Westside area. Do you have 
          a minute?"

Lead:     "Yeah, sure. We're thinking about moving in the 
          next few months."

AgentSix: "That's exciting! Are you looking to buy, or are 
          you exploring your options?"

Lead:     "Definitely buying. We've been pre-approved already."

AgentSix: "Perfect. I'd love to connect you with one of our 
          agents who specializes in Westside. Would tomorrow 
          at 2pm work for a quick call?"

Lead:     "That works."

AgentSix: "Great! I've scheduled that for you. You'll receive 
          a confirmation text shortly. Is there anything 
          specific you'd like our agent to know before the call?"

Lead:     "We need a 4 bedroom minimum."

AgentSix: "Got it - 4 bedrooms minimum. I've noted that. 
          Looking forward to helping you find your perfect home!"
```

### Objection Handling

| Objection | AgentSix Response |
|-----------|-------------------|
| "I already have an agent" | "That's great! Just wanted to make sure you're getting the best options. If anything changes, we're here to help." |
| "I'm just looking" | "Totally understand - most of our clients start that way. Would you like me to send you new listings that match your criteria? No pressure at all." |
| "Call me back later" | "Absolutely! When would be a better time? I'll make a note to reach out then." |
| "How did you get my number?" | "You inquired about a property on [source], and I'm following up to help you. Would you prefer I remove you from our list?" |
| "Is this a robot?" | "Ha! I get that a lot. I'm Sarah, part of the team here. How can I help you today?" |

### Voice Characteristics
- **Speed**: 1.08x natural (slightly faster = more professional)
- **Temperature**: 0.35 (consistent but natural)
- **Responsiveness**: 0.9 (quick replies, no awkward pauses)
- **Backchannel**: ON ("yeah", "mhm", "gotcha" - natural conversation)
