## v2 Features (Only 3)

### Feature 1: AI Voice Outreach + Response (THE WEDGE)
- Inbound: AI picks up calls, qualifies, books to calendar
- Outbound: AI calls opted-in leads, qualifies, books
- Every call transcribed, scored, pushed to dashboard
- **REPLACES:** $795-3,500/mo ISA service

### Feature 2: Buyer Lead Instant Response
- Webhook receiver from Zillow, Realtor.com, Facebook, website
- AI responds in <10 seconds via SMS + email
- Qualifies: timeline, budget, motivation, decision maker
- Books appointments directly via Calendly/Google Calendar
- **REPLACES:** Manual response, VA time

### Feature 3: Compliance Infrastructure (THE MOAT)
- A2P 10DLC registration per agent
- DNC scrubbing (federal + state + internal)
- TCPA litigator list exclusion
- Consent landing pages with timestamped records
- Automated STOP/HELP handling within 10 seconds
- Quiet hours enforcement (8am-9pm recipient local)
- Tech E&O insurance with TCPA endorsement
- **REPLACES:** Legal risk, potential $500K+ lawsuits

**DOUBLE DOWN if:**
- 10+ clients retained
- Net contribution > $5K/mo
- 5+ clients would write referral

**PIVOT if:**
- Voice quality is problem → compliance-only + SMS

- Under 5 clients after 90 days
