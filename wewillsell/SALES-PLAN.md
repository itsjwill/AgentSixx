## Phase 1: Segmentation (Day 1)

### Segment by Deal Volume (Priority Order)
| Segment | Criteria | Why Priority |
|---------|----------|--------------|
| **A-List** | 50+ deals/year | Can afford $1,197/mo, need automation most |
| **B-List** | 25-50 deals/year | Sweet spot - growing, need efficiency |
| **C-List** | 10-25 deals/year | Price sensitive, start with Starter plan |
| **D-List** | <10 deals/year | Low priority - may not afford |

### Segment by Engagement
- **Hot**: Replied to emails last 30 days
- **Warm**: Opened emails last 60 days  
- **Cold**: No engagement 60+ days

### Target First: A-List + Hot/Warm = ~500-800 agents
